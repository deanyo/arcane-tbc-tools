Arcane Burn Planner

Optimal cooldown overlays for TBC Arcane mages — Anniversary realms, patch 2.4.3.

Enter your gear (spell damage, crit, haste rating) and the fight length, tick the cooldowns you bring, and pin anything the raid controls — Bloodlust, Drums, Power Infusion — to the second it's called. Turn on Complicated fight to lay out intermissions, burn, or AoE phases. Then Find optimal overlay: the planner schedules every unpinned cooldown for maximum damage and prints the press-by-press plan, a burn timeline, and a copy-as-text line to follow in the raid. The Boss presets up top load the real current-phase encounters; Debugging presets are sim-verified reference fights, and you can save your own as Custom presets.

Warcraft Logs — kill times, your stats, review a kill

Talks to the Warcraft Logs API directly: guild kill times (with when Bloodlust actually went out) feed the kill-time explorer, My stats fills the gear inputs from your latest logged kill, and clicking any fetched kill reviews it — your actual presses scored against the optimal plan. Needs a free API client from warcraftlogs.com/api/clients — credentials stay in this browser's localStorage only. Anyone with your secret can spend your API quota, so don't reuse a shared one.

Cap sheet — 3-stack Arcane Blast cast time per buff combo

Every combination (up to 3 at a time) of your enabled haste buffs, at your gear rating. “Rating to cap” is how much more gear haste would put that combo at the 1.0s GCD floor — capped means the combo is already there.
